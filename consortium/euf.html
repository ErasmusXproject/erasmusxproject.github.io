European University Foundation (EUF)

The European University Foundation - Campus Europae, more commonly called European University Foundation (EUF) is a network of 19 universities established in 15 countries. It stands for diversity and social fairness in Higher Education (HE) and aims to accelerate the modernisation of the European Higher Education Area. The network deploys intensive cooperation and policy experimentation under five key pillars:

  1. Digital Higher Education both for governance and provision of education,
  2. entrepreneurship and employability skills of graduates,
  3. policy innovation at national and European level,
  4. active citizenship of students and
  5. quality mobility for all.

The EUF will be in charge of IO1 for the ErasmusX project. For more information, please visit the website: www.uni-foundation.eu

Alba Huertas is Policy and Project Coordinator at the EUF. She holds a double five-years degree in Sociology and Political Sciences, and a Master Degree in European Affairs delivered by the University of Paris-Sorbonne. Ms. Huertas expertise focus in the fields of Education, ICT & Innovation. Prior to joining the EUF, she worked for several years as a Communication and Project Manager within a multinational firm in Paris, and gained extensive experience on Policy and Research by working within national, European and International institutions such the Hungarian Academy of Sciences, the UNESCO Education Sector, and the European Committee of the Regions.
