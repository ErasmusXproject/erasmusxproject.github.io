About OT4ME!

About OT4ME!

OT4ME! 2021 is the 1st conference on online teaching for mobile education, organized as a final event of the ErasmusX project, funded by the Erasmus+ Programme together with Sepie.

The objective is to provide an open and international environment for teachers to share their best online practices.

The conference will consist of a set of keynotes and article presentations. All authors are encourage to submit their original articles and they will be published in IEEE Xplore for free. The conference will have no registration fee either.

Finally, if you have any suggestions or want to contribute to the conference somehow, please feel free to contact us via email.

    F.A.Q

    • Is the conference completely free?

      Yes! Completely free. No registration fee and, if your paper is selected, it will also be published for free in IEEE Xplore with a DOI.

    • How do I submit my paper?

      The submission is performed via EDAS ### and you should use the IEEE template. Please check the "Call for Papers!" section for full info.

    • Can I attend the conference if I don't submit any paper?

      Sure! And we encourage to do so and participate as much as you can.

    • Can I participate as reviewer or present a keynote (as a speaker)?

      Yes. However, your CV should be checked before, as we can only select a few candidates for this purpose. Please feel free to reach us via email for additional instructions.

    ONLINE COURSES FOR MOBILE STUDENTS (ErasmusX) is a European KA203 project coordinated by The University of Alcalá devised to offer students the possibility to increase the impact and add professional value to the Erasmus period abroad by combining certified on-line academic studies with professional/sport/social experience overseas. This new scheme supports study + placement mobility as an additional value to the current internships being done abroad..
