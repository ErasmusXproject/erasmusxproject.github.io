About OT4ME!

OT4ME! 2021 is the 1st conference on online teaching for mobile education, organized as a final event of the ErasmusX project, funded by the Erasmus+ Programme together with Sepie.

The objective is to provide an open and international environment for teachers to share their best online practices.

The conference will consist of a set of keynotes and article presentations. All authors are encouraged to submit their original articles and they will be published in the conference proceedings for free (accepted papers will be submitted for inclusion into IEEE Xplore subject to meeting IEEE Xplore’s scope and quality requirements).
The conference will have no registration fee either.

Finally, if you have any suggestions or want to contribute to the conference somehow, please feel free to contact us via email.

    Call for Papers!

    OT4ME! 2021 seeks for original, unpublished work in the field of education, not currently under review by other conferences or journals. Papers submitted to OT4ME! 2021 will be assessed based on originality, technical soundness, clarity and interest to a wide audience.

    Important dates:
    Article submission due: September 2, 2021 (firm deadline) ⬤ Article review feedback: September 27, 2021 ⬤ Final articles due: October 18, 2021

    Three types of submissions are expected: full (up to 8 pages), short (up to 5 pages) and tiny (up to 2 pages). Accepted papers will be submitted for inclusion into IEEE Xplore subject to meeting IEEE Xplore’s scope and quality requirements.
    One best paper distinction will be awarded per each of the three types of submissions.

    All submissions must be written in English and must follow the standard IEEE two-column conference template, available for download here: IEEE templates
    Paper will be submitted only in PDF format via EDAS through this link: https://edas.info/N28697

    Areas of interest and topics include, but are not limited to:

  • E-learning/Online teaching/education ⬤ Erasmus+ Mobility Program ⬤ ICT in Education (infrastructures, software tools, data analytics, etc.) ⬤ Higher Education ⬤ Adult Education ⬤ STEM/STEAM Education ⬤ Global Issues in Education and Research ⬤ Inclusive Education ⬤ Learning/Teaching Methodologies and Assessment ⬤ Course Management ⬤ Curriculum Development ⬤ Competence Development ⬤ Educational Foundations ⬤ Education Policy and Leadership ⬤ Active methodologies (Flipped Classroom, Gamification, etc.) ⬤ Business and Education ⬤ Case studies ⬤ Creative track ⬤

    F.A.Q

    • Is the conference completely free?

      Yes! Completely free. No registration fee and, if your paper is selected, it will be part of the conference proceedings.

    • How do I submit my paper?

      The submission is performed via EDAS at https://edas.info/N28697 and you should use the IEEE template. Please check the "Call for Papers!" section for full info.

    • Can I attend the conference if I don't submit any paper?

      Sure! And we encourage to do so and participate as much as you can.

    • Can I participate as reviewer or present a keynote (as a speaker)?

      Yes. However, your CV should be checked before, as we can only select a few candidates for this purpose. Please feel free to reach us via email for additional instructions.
