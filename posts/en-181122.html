ErasmusX Kick-Off Meeting

On 22-11-2018, the ErasmusX kick-off took place and all the members of the consortium had the opportunity to meet face to face after several virtual conferences.

During the two-day meeting, the members of the group not only introduced their institutions and main actions, such as the creation of online courses, user guides, main catalogue and central platform, etc. but also discussed specific concerns related to legal and organizational requirements and procedures, budget and management, travel requirements and events, methodology, online courses trainers and tutors, ICT tools to be used along the project (Trello, Hangouts, GDrive, OneDrive), quality and dissemination plan.

There was also time to enjoy the views and food in town and to go back in history through a guided tour at Universidad de Alcala, the host institution.
