II Virtual Meeting: ERASMUSX “ONLINE COURSES FOR MOBILE STUDENTS” in #ErasmusDays 2020

As participant of #ErasmusDays 2020 “Virtual open house”, organized for the upcoming 15, 16 and 17th of October, ErasmusX invites the HE community to join again online on 17th October at 17:00 CEST via this Hangouts link to meet the team and get informed about the project and/or how to participate in the future.

In this II Virtual meeting, ErasmusX will present their project development. Members of the 5 European institutions (Universidad de Alcalá, University of Napoli Federico II, Lodz University of Technology, University of Latvia and European University Foundation) will not only introduce their universities, but also the programs they offer online, the methodology and the digital tools to be used in online teaching.

We look forward to meeting you! :)

  • For additional information, please visit the official website: https://www.erasmusdays.eu/
