ErasmusX in the XII EIDU conference

The EIDU conference is organized every year by the University of Alcala, in this XII EIDU edition the main talks were focused on online teaching practices and the conference was completely virtual via the Blackboard / AulaVirtual platform, celebrated during two weeks and ending next Friday Oct 23rd.

Oue ErasmusX team, particularly the members from University of Alcala, presented a set of ICT resources learned from the ErasmusX project that was very well received, with several interactions with other professors from different teaching fields.
