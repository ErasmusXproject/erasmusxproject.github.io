December 8, 2020

Presenting ErasmusX instructional design at ECONF20

On 6th and 7th December 2020, the ErasmusX team presented their project and common instructional design method for online course methodology in the 6th Conference on e-learning (eConf20).

The conference was organized by University of Barhain and design models for information systems were discussed.

Gratitude to the organizers, the attendees and the chairman Dr. Ehab Jum Adwan for his valuable expertise and feedback on Case Study procedures during the discussion and to University of Bahrain for hosting such an interdisciplinary and international event with more than 250 proposals. We are proud to be among the 80 ones selected!
