#ErasmusDays: III Virtual Meeting: ErasmusX "Online Courses for Mobile Students"

As participant of #ErasmusDays 2021 “Virtual open house”, ErasmusX invites the HE community to join on 14-10-21 at 14:00 via Hangouts. ErasmusX will present the program offered by 5 European institutions (Alcalá University, University of Napoli Federico II, Lodz University of Technology, Latvia University & EUF) and the courses they offer online. Further information about the conference OT4ME! supported by ErasmusX will be also provided.

This ErasmusX event has been selected as an example by our National Agencies, SEPIE and INJUVE-ANE.

Hope to see you soon there! :)
