December 31, 2021

Project completion and farewell

Officially, today is the last day of our project, ErasmusX. More than three years, a new consortium, travelling, a course catalog, and even a pandemic! :)

We are proud of the results yielded by our project. ErasmusX project finishes on 31 December 2021, but our website will be still active. Please feel free to reach us via email. You can also visit the platform with the catalogue and the online courses offered from different European Universities at:

erasmusx.uni-foundation.eu

Finally, we would like to dedicate this post to our excellent ErasmusX consortium: all partners, professors, students, technicians...

...THANK YOU!!!
